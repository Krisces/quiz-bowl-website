Test Yourself


This page will help you practice or prepare yourself for a Quiz Bowl Tournament with some resources from the National Academic Quiz Tournaments (NAQT), LLC.

This quiz game will be testing your knowledge with a timer. However, bonus questions will not be included. Only Tossups will be tested. If you know the answer to the questions, just click the "Buzz" button. You will then have 15 seconds with a visible timer to write down your answer. If the answer is correct, you will receive 10 points. If the answer is incorrect, the game will move on to the next question.

You can chose whether you want to test yourself by category or simulate a tournament with randomized questions.

If requested, I can include bonus questions in the future too.
